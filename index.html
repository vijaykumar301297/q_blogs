Breaking Down the Platform's Powerhouse Features:

The Connective Automation Platform is like a well-oiled machine, with each component working seamlessly together to drive automation success. Let's delve into its key sections:

1. Process Understanding:

Shine a light on your processes: Process Intelligence use data from Process mining, Process Capture, certify automation to automatically map out your existing processes, giving you a clear picture of what's happening behind the scenes.

Identify Automation opportunities: Once you understand your processes, you can pinpoint bottlenecks and areas for automation. This lets you target your efforts where they'll have the biggest impact.

Create a blueprint for success: By documenting your business processes in a standardized way, you can ensure consistency and make it easier to automate tasks in the future. Business Process Documents could be used for auditing, knowledge transfer, and training.

2. Test Automation:

Say goodbye to manual testing: Worksoft's codeless approach lets you record your actions as you walk through a process, automatically converting them into reusable test artifact. This saves you time and effort and makes it easier to keep your tests up to date.

Built-in resilience: Changes in your applications won't break your tests. Worksoft patented object-action framework adapts to changes, ensuring your tests are always reliable.

Comprehensive coverage: Whether you're testing web, mobile, or desktop applications, Worksoft has you covered. The platform supports a wide range of technologies and applications.

3. RPA:

Automate the mundane: Free your employees from repetitive tasks like data entry and form filling with RPA robots. These software robots can handle these tasks 24/7, with accuracy and efficiency.

Boost productivity and compliance: RPA can significantly improve your team's output and ensure that your processes are always compliant with regulations.
